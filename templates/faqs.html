Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website is a digital playground where my creative projects run wild. Think of it as my personal corner of the internet where curiosity meets technology, and they have a great time together.

2. How can I contact you for collaboration or inquiries?

You can contact me by sending a message through the "Contact" form on this website. If that fails, try a message in a bottle, though the response time may vary.

3. What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in making computers do things they probably don’t want to, like analyzing data, creating visual reports, and detecting oil spills from outer space. I’m basically a digital cat herder.

4. Are your projects open source?

Yes! My projects are as open as a coffee shop at 8 AM. You can find them on GitHub, where they're hanging out, waiting for someone to buy them a metaphorical latte.

6. Can I find a unicorn on this website?

Only if you believe hard enough. But seriously, if you find one, let me know—I've been looking for it for ages. I suspect it's hiding somewhere in the code.

7. If I hit a bug, what should I do?

Firstly, don’t panic. Offer it some coffee; bugs love coffee. If that doesn’t work, report it through the contact form, and I’ll come to the rescue with my digital flyswatter.
